Voices of NIST

Our Family

We all have stories to tell, stories that reveal who we are, and the experiences and people that have shaped us. They allow us to reflect, learn and connect. Our community believes that sharing these stories empowers us and others, extending our vision far beyond the walls of our school.

The Voices of NIST represent only a few of the thousands of students, parents and teachers who have helped build a remarkable community, one that continues to inspire us and drive us to excel even further. Through their contributions we have all been enriched.
Wanpen Thesthong
